About

FlexiDAO provides the industry’s first blockchain and cloud-based energy services aggregation platform .

Combined with our innovative smart charger – the smartest and cheapest EV charger in the market -, FlexiDAO’s platform can control and optimize EV charging providing extra income for the drivers and helping energy companies to stabilize the grid and consume more green energy.

Leveraging blockchain technology capabilities, we can eliminate nearly all administrative and financial expenses and offer to our customers higher rewards and the highest security available in the market .
FlexiDAO's platform is extremely interoperable and it can integrate EV manufacturers, other EVSE OEMs, utilities, and other commercial energy partners.
