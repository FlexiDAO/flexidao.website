About us

Who we are:
FlexiDAO provides ethereum blockchain-enabled smart solutions to unlock the potential of residential demand side response.

Need:
As generation decentralizes, renewables expand and digitalization makes grids smarter, grid operators need increasingly flexible balancing loads. However, the high costs of residential demand side response hamper the integration of residential loads into balancing markets.

Solution:
Residential EV chargers with blockchain capabilities that can coordinate the charging of the cars to help balancing the grid, while generating extra income for the driver. Using blockchain, we can efficiently and cheaply aggregate separate loads (EVs) turning them into one single flexible load that can be traded on the market.

Vision:
FlexiDAO aims to provide more utility to the energy system by aggregating and making use of energy resources at residential level like car batteries, home batteries, HVAC systems and other flexible loads.
