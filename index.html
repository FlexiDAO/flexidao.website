About

Our mission is to provide an innovative, sustainable and affordable
solution to businesses, communities and public entities to encourage the adoption of electric vehicles while allowing more efficient utilization of existing grid infrastructure.

Features

By controlling and optimizing EV charging, we provide extra income
for the EV chargers’ owners while helping energy companies to stabilize the grid and consume more green energy.

Effortless EV management &
load management.

Site owners can choose the access policy to their chargers (e.g. free, controlled or paid access) depending on the type of user. The platform makes sure that your electrical infrastructure supports your EV charging needs without extra costs.

More green energy,
less CO2 emissions.

You will limit utilization of expensive and polluting peak power plants, and allow integration of more renewable energy into the system. Or, you can also integrate your own onsite generation and energy management systems.

Lower chargers’
ownership costs.

Thanks to our underlying advanced technology, we can eliminate nearly all administrative and financial costs and offer higher rewards. Combined with the savings of load balancing, the charging network ownership costs will be lower than ever.

« The only aspects of the smart grid that can be truly smart are the people within it »

Technology

We provide the industry’s first blockchain and cloud-based energy services aggregation platform to enable collaboration of businesses, EV owners and energy companies.

Open and collaborative
platform

Leveraging blockchain technology, the platform is hosted and sustained by its own users offering unprecendented active collaboration.

High performance
and reliability.

The platform is designed with an interlinked blockchain construction: scheduling and aggregation occurs over the FlexiDAO blockchain, while value storage and exchange is externalized to public Ethereum chain.

Interoperable and
scalable network

The platform can easily integrate different ev-chargers manufacturers, other distributed loads (i.e. home-batteries), utilities, and other commercial energy partners.
