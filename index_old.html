About us

Who we are:
FlexiDAO provides Electric Vehicle chargers with blockchain capabilities that can manage an aggregated fleet of EVs providing an adjustable load for grid operators and extra income for drivers. The project is beeing developed in collaboration with UPC by Grzegorz Bytniewski and Simone Accornero.

Mission:
FlexiDAO turns wide-spread EV roll-out from a problem into a solution. The driver gets paid for smart charging of the vehicle while the grid operator benefits from residential active load management (DSR).

Vision:
FlexiDAO aims to provide more utility to the energy system by aggregating and making use of energy resources at residential level like car batteries, home batteries, HVAC systems and other flexible loads.
